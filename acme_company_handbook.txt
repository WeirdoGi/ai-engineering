ACME TECHNOLOGIES — COMPANY HANDBOOK
Last updated: Q1 2025

---

ABOUT ACME TECHNOLOGIES

Acme Technologies was founded in 2018 by CEO Sarah Chen and CTO Marcus Webb in Austin, Texas. The company builds enterprise workflow automation software for mid-size manufacturing companies. Acme currently employs 142 people across offices in Austin, Chicago, and a remote team spanning 12 countries.

In 2024, Acme reached $18.4 million in annual recurring revenue, representing 47% year-over-year growth. The company raised a $22 million Series B in March 2024 led by Sequoia Capital.

---

PRODUCTS

Acme Flow — the core product. A no-code workflow automation platform that connects manufacturing equipment, ERP systems, and team communication tools. Launched in 2019, Acme Flow currently serves 340 enterprise customers.

Acme Insights — a real-time analytics dashboard launched in 2022 that sits on top of Acme Flow data. Allows plant managers to monitor production efficiency, flag bottlenecks, and forecast output. Priced at $800/month per facility.

Acme Connect — an API layer launched in 2023 that allows customers to build custom integrations between Acme Flow and third-party tools. Used by 89 customers as of Q4 2024.

TEAM STRUCTURE

Engineering — 54 engineers split across four squads: Platform, Integrations, Analytics, and Infrastructure. VP of Engineering is David Park, who joined from Stripe in 2022.

Sales — 28 person team led by Chief Revenue Officer Priya Nair. Average deal size is $42,000 ARR. Sales cycle is typically 45-90 days.

Customer Success — 19 person team led by Maya Torres. Net revenue retention is 118%, meaning existing customers expand their contracts faster than churn occurs.

Product — 8 person team led by CPO James Liu. Acme ships on a 3-week sprint cycle with a major release every quarter.

Marketing — 12 person team led by CMO Rachel Kim. Primary channels are content marketing, industry conferences, and a partner referral program.

BENEFITS AND COMPENSATION

Health Insurance — Acme covers 100% of employee premiums for medical, dental, and vision. Dependents are covered at 80%.

Equity — all full-time employees receive stock options. Standard vesting is 4 years with a 1-year cliff.

PTO — unlimited PTO policy with a mandatory minimum of 15 days per year. The company shuts down the last week of December each year.

Remote Work — employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week. Fully remote roles are available for Engineering and Customer Success.

Learning Budget — every employee receives $1,500 per year for courses, conferences, books, and professional development.

401k — Acme matches 4% of salary with immediate vesting.

Parental Leave — 16 weeks fully paid for all parents regardless of gender.

---

ENGINEERING STANDARDS

Language and frameworks — backend services are written in Python (FastAPI) and Go. Frontend is React with TypeScript. Mobile is React Native.

Deployment — all services run on AWS. Infrastructure is managed with Terraform. CI/CD runs on GitHub Actions.

Code review — all pull requests require at least two approvals. Engineers are expected to review within 24 hours of being tagged.

On-call — each squad rotates on-call weekly. On-call engineers are expected to respond to P1 incidents within 15 minutes.

Testing — minimum 80% unit test coverage required before merging. Integration tests run on every PR.

Documentation — all new features require a technical design doc before development begins. Docs live in Notion.

COMPANY VALUES

Move fast with care — Acme ships quickly but takes quality seriously. Speed and quality are not trade-offs.

Customer obsession — every decision starts with the customer. Engineers are encouraged to join customer calls.

Transparency — financials, roadmap, and company strategy are shared with all employees quarterly.

Ownership — employees are expected to own problems end to end, not hand things off and forget them.

Diversity and inclusion — Acme has a dedicated DEI committee and publishes an annual diversity report.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: How do I request time off?
A: Submit PTO requests in Rippling at least 5 business days in advance for periods longer than 3 days. For single days, notify your manager via Slack.

Q: Who do I contact for IT issues?
A: Submit a ticket in the #it-help Slack channel. Response time is within 2 hours during business hours.

Q: How does the performance review process work?
A: Acme runs bi-annual reviews in June and December. Each review includes a self-assessment, peer feedback from 3 colleagues, and a 1:1 with your manager. Compensation adjustments happen in the December cycle.

Q: How do I submit an expense report?
A: Submit expenses in Expensify within 30 days of the purchase. Expenses over $500 require manager approval before purchase.

Q: What is the policy on side projects?
A: Employees may work on side projects as long as they do not compete with Acme's business or use Acme's proprietary information. Notify your manager if you begin a side project.
